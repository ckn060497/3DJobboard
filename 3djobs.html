        Why Vertex exists

        Job boards flattened everything into the same list. We put the shape back in.

        Vertex started as a side project by three engineers tired of skimming identical postings. We rebuilt the listing itself — depth, tilt, layers — so a role actually looks like what it is before you commit to reading it.

        Signal over noise

        Every listing carries real comp, real team size, and a real requirement list — no vague "competitive salary."

        Depth over distance

        We'd rather show you fewer roles clearly than bury a good fit in a thousand near-identical rows.

        Speed for both sides

        Applying and reviewing happen in the same place, so good matches move in hours, not weeks.
